Recipe Details

25 min 4 servings 420 cal
Italian Vegetarian Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add slices of mozzarella and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
  7. Let cool for 2 minutes before slicing and serving.
