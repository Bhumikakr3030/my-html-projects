Common Symptoms

Fever

High temperature is one of the most common symptoms of COVID-19.

Dry Cough

Persistent dry cough that doesn't produce mucus.

Fatigue

Extreme tiredness and lack of energy.

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing or feeling breathless.

Loss of Taste/Smell

Sudden loss of taste or smell without nasal congestion.

Headache & Body Aches

Persistent headaches and muscle or body aches.

Prevention Measures

Wash Hands

Wash your hands regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Wear Mask

Wear a mask in public places and when around people who don't live with you.

Social Distance

Maintain at least 6 feet distance from others in public spaces.

Stay Home

Stay home when you're sick and avoid unnecessary outings.

Get Vaccinated

Get vaccinated and stay up to date with booster shots when eligible.

Sanitize

Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.
